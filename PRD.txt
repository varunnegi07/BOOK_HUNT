📘 Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Product Name: Book Hunt
1. Product Overview
Book Hunt is an AI-powered educational platform designed for school and college students to easily find, study, and interact with academic books relevant to their courses.
The platform helps students:
* Discover books related to their school class or college course
* Summarize books
* Ask questions from the book
* Learn through an AI chatbot trained on the book’s content
The goal is to make studying simpler, interactive, and personalized.
________________


2. Problem Statement
Students often face these problems:
* Hard to find the right books for their course
* Books are too long and difficult to understand
* No quick way to ask questions from the book
* Searching information in a book is time consuming
Book Hunt solves this by providing:
✔ Personalized book recommendations
✔ AI-powered book assistant
✔ Instant summaries
✔ Interactive learning
________________

5. Core Features
5.1 User Signup & Login
Users can create an account using:
* Google Login
* Email + Password
Signup Form Fields
* Name
* Age
* Institution Name
* School / College selection
* Class or Course
* Year/Semester
________________


6. User Type Selection
After signup users must choose:
Option 1: School
Fields:
* School Name
* Class
* Board (CBSE / ICSE / State)
Option 2: College
Fields:
* College Name
* Course (B.Tech, BBA, BSc etc.)
* Branch / Major
* Year/Semester
This helps the AI recommend relevant books.
